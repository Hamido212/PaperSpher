## Brieffix — No More Paperwork

Brieffix is a German-language SaaS that uses AI to generate formal, ready-to-send letters in seconds. Users describe their situation in plain language — no legal jargon needed. The AI produces a legally sound letter that can be printed, copied, or sent immediately.

### 13 Categories

- **Cancellations** — Subscriptions, gym memberships, streaming, internet contracts
- **Revocations** — Online purchases and contracts
- **Travel Law** — Compensation claims for flights, trains, and package tours
- **Tenancy Law** — Security deposit recovery, reporting defects
- **Objections** — Correcting wrong invoices
- **Job Applications** — Professional cover letters
- And more — including a free-form mode

### By the Numbers

- Thousands of letters generated
- < 1 minute to first draft
- Free to start — no subscription required

### Tech Stack

Next.js · TypeScript · Shadcn/UI · Brevo (transactional email) · Vercel
